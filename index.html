About CLC e-CAST

e-CAST is a national level scholarship cum admission test conducted online by CLC. It offers a window of opportunity to students to be at par, and be one among the best engineering minds.

Scope

e-CAST is not just another scholarship exam to help reduce financial burden, it offers an insight into what goes into making the toppers of JEE and NEET Services Exam. The online platform used in e-CAST matches the orientation and level of top level competitive exams.

Exam Pattern

Mode : Online

Time Duration : 2 Hr.

Total Marks : 400

Total Question : 100 (4 Marks for each carrect answer)

Negative Marking : -1 marks will awarded for each wrong answer

Test can be written from anywhere using system (Desktop, Laptop) and mobile.

Instructions

The e-CAST exam will start on 30th Jan, 2020 at 10:00 AM sharp. Students will not be allowed to take the e-CAST Exam after 12 PM.

Make sure that you have a computer system (desktop/laptop) with updated windows version and google chrome

Before taking the e-CAST, make sure that your Internet connectivity is not interrupted during the exam.

The candidate has the flexibility to jump over to any question while writing e-CAST.

If a candidate fails to complete the e-CAST exam in allotted time, then his test will get submitted automatically.
